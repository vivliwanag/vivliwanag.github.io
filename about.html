Artwork

Creating visual art pieces has always been a passion of mine since I remember picking up a pencil for the first time. I love drawing realism portraits and compositions of everyday life. I first started out with traditional painting and colouring but during the pandemic I ventured out into creating digital artworks. During the pandemic I completed a challenge of creating an artwork a day for 30 days straight. Most of my artworks included are from the time of completing the challenge.

Photography

Photography is one of many artforms I enjoy. I was 12 years old when I first worked professionally as a photographer for my dad's photography and DJing company. I was his second photographer for a wedding at the Old Mill. My favourite genre of photography is street photography because it captures the raw and candid life of the city.
